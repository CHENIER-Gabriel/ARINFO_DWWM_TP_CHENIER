AKA

Singer/Illustrator

AKA is a virtual singer from France with emotional and elegant tones.
Active on the internet since 2011, she sings original songs and covers in Japanese, French, English and coin words in the doujin and rhythm games sphere.
She's an android witch traveling across dimensions in search for her memories fragments scattered in music.
